1. Introduction

Welcome to betterday.

betterday is a voice-first reflection app that helps you understand your days, your mood, and your patterns over time. This privacy policy explains:

  • What data we collect
  • How we use it
  • With whom we share it
  • Your choices and rights

When we say “we”, “our”, or “betterday”, we mean the betterday app and its individual developer(s).

When we say “you”, we mean you, the user of the app.

By using betterday, you agree to this privacy policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the app.

2. High-level summary (TL;DR)

  • We collect only the data needed to provide the app: authentication, your daily entries, moods, and AI-generated insights.
  • Your content is stored in secure cloud storage (Firebase / Google Cloud) linked to your account via an internal user ID.
  • When we call external AI/LLM services, we send only the journal content itself (voice and/or text) using our own API credentials.
    • We do not include your betterday user ID, email address, or login tokens in those AI requests.
    • The AI provider sees our project/account, not your individual identity.
  • Once we receive the AI results (transcript, detected activities, insights), we store them in Firebase and associate them with your internal user ID. That association happens only in our database.
  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not share your data with advertisers or use it for cross-app tracking.
  • You can delete your account and associated data from inside the app (or by contacting us).

The sections below explain everything in more detail.

3. What data we collect

3.1 Account and authentication data

When you sign in with Apple or Google (via Firebase Authentication), we receive:

  • A unique user identifier (UID) from Firebase
  • Basic profile information from the sign-in provider, such as:
    • Display name (if available)
    • Email address (depending on provider settings and what you consent to share)

betterday itself primarily uses:

  • The UID to associate entries and insights to your account
  • Optionally, a nickname you choose

We do not store your password. Passwords are handled by Apple, Google, or Firebase Authentication.

In some situations (for testing or support), we may allow sign-in via email and password using Firebase Authentication. In such cases, password handling is managed by Firebase; we do not see or store your password in plain text.

3.2 Journal content and activity data

To provide the core functionality of betterday, we collect and store:

  • Voice recordings of your daily reflections (locally, on device)
  • Transcripts of what you said (text generated from your audio)
  • Activities and tags extracted by AI from your text (e.g. work, exercise, social, rest)
  • Your selected mood for each day (e.g. good/okay/low/bad, depending on the version)
  • Any routines you input manually in settings
  • AI-generated insights, such as:
    • Patterns over weeks or months (e.g. which activities correlate with better or worse mood)

While you are speaking, betterday may also use Apple’s on-device speech recognition to process your voice in real time. This on-device recognition is used to:

  • Detect specific words during the recording
  • Mark routines or goals you have previously configured in the app
  • Give you a rough, immediate tracking of what you are mentioning as you talk

This on-device recognition happens locally on your iPhone and is separate from the later AI/LLM calls we make to external services.

We also store:

  • Timestamps for each entry (date and time of the reflection)
  • Basic contextual metadata like your time zone, to correctly align days and periods

These data are necessary for betterday to:

  • Show you your history
  • Calculate streaks
  • Generate daily and monthly insights

When we later send your journal content to external AI/LLM services, we send only the anonymized text of the entry itself. The link between that content and your betterday account (your internal user ID and any associated email) is created only when we store the AI results back into our own database.

3.3 Technical & usage data

To keep betterday secure and functioning, we may collect limited technical data, such as:

  • App version and platform (e.g. betterday version X.X on iOS)
  • Basic log information when errors occur (e.g. failed requests, timeouts)
  • Information about the Firebase or AI request that failed (non-sensitive technical details)

We do not collect this information for advertising.

It is used for debugging, improving reliability, and understanding high-level usage (for example, how often features break).

We do not currently integrate third-party advertising SDKs or general marketing analytics SDKs.

3.4 Support and communication data

If you contact us by email or other channels, we collect:

  • Your email address
  • The content of your message
  • Any screenshots or logs you choose to send

We use this information only to:

  • Respond to your questions
  • Investigate bugs
  • Improve the app based on your feedback

4. How we use your data

We use the data described above to:

  1. Provide the service
    • Authenticate you and keep you signed in
    • Store and retrieve your entries, moods, activities, and insights
    • Calculate streaks and show your history
    • Generate summaries and monthly patterns
  2. Run AI-powered features
    • Transcribe your audio into text
    • Extract activities and categories from your text
    • Compute insights over time (e.g. patterns, correlations)
  3. Maintain and improve betterday
    • Diagnose bugs and performance issues
    • Understand, in aggregate, how features are used
    • Prioritize improvements based on anonymized patterns
  4. Communicate with you
    • Respond to support requests and feedback
    • Inform you of important changes to the app or this policy (when appropriate)
  5. Comply with law
    • Respond to valid legal requests if required
    • Enforce our terms and prevent abuse

We do not use your data to build advertising profiles. We do not conduct interest-based advertising.

5. How AI and LLM processing works

betterday uses external AI/LLM services via API to power core features, including:

  • Speech-to-text for full transcription of your recording (separate from Apple’s on-device speech recognition used during live speaking)
  • Extracting activities and categories from your text
  • Generating insights and summaries for chosen periods (e.g. a month)

In practice:

  1. You press record and speak about your day.
  2. While you speak, Apple’s on-device speech recognition may run locally to detect certain routine-related words in real time.
  3. The app sends your journal content (audio and text) securely to an external AI service using our own API credentials.
  4. The AI service:
    • Converts your speech into a full transcript
    • Analyzes the text to identify activities, themes, and patterns
  5. The results (transcript, activities, insights) are returned to betterday.
  6. betterday then stores these results in Firebase and links them to your internal user ID in our database so you can see them in your history and insights.

What we send (and what we avoid sending)

  • We send the content of your journal that is needed for transcription and analysis (your spoken text and its derived text).
  • We do not include your betterday user ID, your email address, or your login tokens in the AI request payload when it is technically possible to avoid it.
  • The AI provider sees that the request comes from our betterday project/account, not from you as an individually-identifiable user.

Keep in mind that the content of your journal itself may contain personal information (names, places, events) that you choose to speak about. If you are concerned, you should avoid including highly sensitive identifiers (like full addresses, ID numbers, or other people’s personal data) in your entries.

How AI providers handle data

  • Your journal content is processed by third-party AI providers under their own terms and privacy policies.
  • They may temporarily store logs to operate, secure, and improve their services, depending on their configuration and legal obligations.
  • We do not allow these providers to use your data for targeted advertising.

The association between the AI output (transcripts, activities, insights) and your specific betterday account happens only inside our own storage layer (Firebase / Google Cloud) after we receive the response.

6. Where and how we store data

6.1 On your device

Some data is stored locally on your device, for example:

  • Cached copies of your entries or insights
  • Temporary data used during processing

This is controlled by your device’s own security (e.g. passcode, Face ID).

6.2 In the cloud (Firebase / Google Cloud)

The main copy of your account data is stored using Firebase (Google Cloud), which provides:

  • Authentication (sign-in with Apple, Google, or email/password where enabled)
  • Cloud database storage for:
    • Entries
    • Moods
    • Activities
    • Insights
    • Streaks and relevant metadata

Access to these databases is controlled by security rules tied to your authenticated user ID, so that each user can normally access only their own data.

6.3 Retention

We generally keep your data for as long as:

  • You have an active betterday account; and
  • The app needs the data to provide features like history and insights.

You can:

  • Delete individual entries (where supported in the UI).
  • Delete your entire account and associated data (see Your choices & rights below).

After you request deletion, there may be a short period where data remains in backups or logs before it is fully removed, as is standard for cloud services.

7. How we share your data

We do not sell your data.

We may share your data with:

  1. Service providers (data processors)

    Only to the extent necessary to provide betterday. These include:

    • Firebase / Google Cloud (authentication, database, cloud functions)
    • AI / LLM providers (transcription, analysis, insights)
      • These providers process your data on our behalf to deliver the app’s functionality.
  2. Legal and safety reasons

    We may share or preserve information if we reasonably believe it is necessary to:

    • Comply with applicable law, regulation, or legal process
    • Enforce our terms or protect the rights, property, or safety of betterday, our users, or others
  3. Aggregated or de-identified information

    We may use or share aggregated statistics (e.g. how many users use a specific feature) that do not identify you personally.

We do not share your personal data with advertisers or data brokers.

8. Cookies and tracking

betterday is a native iOS app and does not use web cookies.

We do not use your data to track you across other apps or websites for advertising purposes.

Any identifiers used (for example, your Firebase user ID) are used only to:

  • Authenticate you
  • Link your entries and insights to your account
  • Measure app reliability and usage in aggregate (non-advertising purposes)

9. Your choices and rights

9.1 In-app controls

Depending on your version of betterday, you may be able to:

  • Edit or delete individual entries
  • Disable notifications (via iOS system settings)
  • Sign out from your account

9.2 Delete your account

betterday provides an in-app option to delete your account and data:

  1. Open More view
  2. Tap in Settings in betterday.
  3. Tap Delete account & data
  4. Confirm that you want to delete your account.

This typically:

  • Deletes your user record
  • Deletes your entries, moods, and associated AI insights
  • Revokes any Apple or Google access tokens used by betterday.

If you cannot find this option or it fails, email trybetterday@gmail.com from the same email or with enough information to identify your account, and request account deletion.

9.3 Access, correction, and export

  • You can view your entries and insights directly in the app.
  • If you believe some data is incorrect and cannot edit it yourself, contact us and we will attempt to correct it.
  • Export options may be available through the app (e.g. JSON/CSV) as we roll them out during beta (will be implemented in upcoming releases)

If you are in a region with specific legal rights over your personal data (for example, the EU/EEA, UK, or similar jurisdictions), you may have additional rights such as:

  • Right of access to your data
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to deletion (erasure)
  • Right to restrict or object to certain processing
  • Right to data portability

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at trybetterday@gmail.com.

We may need to verify your identity before fulfilling your request.

10. Children’s privacy

betterday is designed for adults and older teenagers who want to reflect on their day. It is not specifically targeted at children under 16.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 16. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at trybetterday@gmail.com so we can delete it.

11. International data transfers

Our service providers (such as Firebase and AI providers) may store and process data in data centers located in different countries.

By using betterday, you understand that your data may be processed outside of your country of residence. We rely on these providers’ security and compliance programs and aim to choose reputable vendors with strong privacy and security standards.
